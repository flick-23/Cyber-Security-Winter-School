About Winter School on Cyber Security

Winter School on Cyber Security is a conference about creating awareness in cyber security and related domains. To provide an opportunity to researchers to get early feedback on their cyber security related research work through poster presentation. This conference also focuses on building practical skills through Capture The Flag (CTF) event and to create an active cyber security group at Institution.
