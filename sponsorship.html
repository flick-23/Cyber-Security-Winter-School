Sponsorship

Sponsors are what make this conference possible. BelPy cannot happen without them, and we are really grateful to all those who have generously come forward to support us. They make it possible for so many to attend, to present their knowledge, and to get the best experience from the conference.

Prospects and Benefits
  • Put your business in the spotlight
    Help your brand gain exposure and elevate your business identity with the Python community. This is your chance to promote your brand and attract passionate people.
  • Visibility
    Your brand logo will be featured on our website, social media, YouTube and other places.
  • Give back to the community
    "There is more happiness in giving, than there is in receiving". After all the challenges we have faced owing to the pandemic, you can help sincere individuals with an eagerness to learn. Sponsorship is a way of giving back to the community and thanking them for their support. Your sponsorship will help us make this conference affordable and accessible to the widest possible audience.
  • Explore talent
    Are you looking for Python developers? What better place to find them, than at a Python conference! Get a chance to explore talented and intelligent minds that many are looking for.
  • Flexibility
    We want both parties to be happy and gain something from this opportunity. If you have something specific you require from us, don't hesitate to ask!
If you are interested in supporting us, please contact us at python.belagavi@gmail.com
